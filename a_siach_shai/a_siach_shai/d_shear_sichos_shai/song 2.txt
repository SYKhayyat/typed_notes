To The Tune of: Ani Maamin (JEP III)
Angels praise Him constantly,
The whole creation gives thanks to Him-
But he wants from you and wants from me-
The shevach we give is so dear!

For nothing comes by our strength or might-
We have to thank Him for all we have-
And though our minds can feel it’s right,
It’s hard to thank Him, I fear.

Chorus.
Time and time again,
We have to thank Hashem-
Everything comes straight from Him-
Thank you Hashem!
End of Chorus.

Mah ashiv laHashem
Kol tagmulohi alay:
Hashem’s so great and we never can
Repay the gifts we’ve received.

All we can do is to try our best
In serving Him and thanking Him;
Try your hardest to pass each test,
Follow his word faithfully.

Chorus.

Recognize he’s providing,
Creating and sustaining.
All comes from shamayim;
All comes straight from Him.

Then take those feeling and sing his praise,
Repay Hashem Hashem with your thank you;
Thank Him in a specific way
For each and every thing.

Chorus.

We daven three times each day,
And often our mind starts to wander-
We’re spending the time anyway,
So why not focus on prayer?!

If we pray with kavanah,
We’ll show Hashem what we think of Him;
Kavanah on his avodah
Will show Him how much we care.

Chorus.

Each day we are commanded
To say one hundred brachos;
Our thank you is expanded
In detail, we must thank Him.

Thanking Him in these special ways
Turns our hearts so close to Him;
We think of Him all throughout the day,
Constantly looking t’wards Him.

Chorus.

The more we gratefully give Him praise,
The more his chessed is given.
So thank Him every single day-
Thank you for all that you give!

Chorus.
