In Lakewood, on the tenth of Av,
I’m getting married! I would love
To see you there – so won’t you come?
I want to dance with everyone!
It’s bein hazmanim, you’re aware,
And you’ll have lots of time to spare!
If you find נכפל במאה amusing, you are definitely a close enough friend to come. If you are reading this sign with a gigantic question mark over your head and a befuddled expression on your face, then I would still love to see you, but you probably need not come.
