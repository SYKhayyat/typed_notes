Directions to Wicheta, Kansas
for Shimon
First, you take the forty-six,
then you take the eight-
then try and find a bathroom quick,
for all the nosh you ate.
Next, you take the twenty-three
to the forty one;
honk at every car you see,
and have a lot of fun!
Take the eighty-seven past
where it hits the three;
try to drive it really fast
(but don’t let Ima see!)
The New Outfit
for Ida
She showed up to the wedding
in a pilgrim dress.
They said she looked so ”heaven“;
they said she looked her best.
She showed up bright and early-
she could not bear to wait-
but it seemed her dress was surely
centuries too late.
A School in Mourning
for Tova
The school’s decked in black,
as it watches her back,
as it slowly recedes in the distance.
It weeps and it cries,
amidst pitiful sighs,
for the things that it lost in that instant
that she made up her mind,
when she left it behind,
when she chose where she would, in this year, go.
And, we think her a fool
for not picking this school-
The Paterson High School for Weirdos.
